Our Mission & Team

TruthLens was built to fight misinformation using advanced AI agents. We believe in a transparent, verified, and trustworthy digital information ecosystem.

Project Idea

A real-time hybrid Fact Checker that combines Web Scraping (DuckDuckGo) with Neural Search (SBERT) and Contextual Analysis. Unlike black-box models, TruthLens provides transparent source-based verification.
